Margarita

A margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, triple sec, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is traditionally served in the eponymous margarita glass, a stepped-diameter variant of a cocktail glass.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz Triple Sec
  • Kosher salt
  • Lime wedge

Recipe

  1. Coat the edge of the glass with salt, add lime wedge.
  2. Stir the lime juice, triple sec, tequila, and ice cubes.
  3. Shake until mixed, strain into glass.

Notes

Some recipes call for an additional teaspoon or so of finely ground sugar. Add to taste or request.
