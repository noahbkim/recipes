Margarita

A margarita is a cocktail consisting of tequila, triple sec, and lime juice often served with salt on the rim of the glass. The drink is traditionally served in the eponymous margarita glass, a stepped-diameter variant of a cocktail glass.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz Triple Sec

Recipe

Notes

Margarita's are a great drink for the summer.
